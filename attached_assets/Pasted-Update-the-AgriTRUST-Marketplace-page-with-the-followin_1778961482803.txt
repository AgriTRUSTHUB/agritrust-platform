## 1. IMAGE GENERATION — USE REPLIT'S BUILT-IN FLUX MODEL

Do NOT use Unsplash, placeholder icons, or any external image API.

Generate every product image using Replit's built-in Flux image 
generation model. Each image must be photorealistic, professionally 
composed, and match the exact product being listed.

Use this prompt structure for every product:

"Professional food photography of [product name], fresh, high quality, 
natural lighting, farm setting, Namibian agricultural context, 
clean background, commercial grade, ultra realistic"

Category-specific prompt guidance:

GRAINS:
"Freshly harvested [grain name], golden tones, rustic wooden surface, 
natural light, Namibian farm, ultra realistic, overhead shot"

VEGETABLES:
"Fresh [vegetable name], vibrant colour, morning light, farm crate 
or market setting, Namibian produce, ultra realistic, commercial grade"

FRUIT:
"Ripe [fruit name], vivid natural colours, outdoor farm setting, 
Namibia, natural sunlight, ultra realistic, professional food photography"

LIVESTOCK:
"Healthy [breed and animal], standing in dry Namibian savanna or 
kraal, golden hour lighting, ultra realistic, commercial farm photography"

DAIRY AND EGGS:
"Fresh [product name], clean rustic surface, natural warm light, 
farm aesthetic, ultra realistic, professional food photography"

HONEY AND ORGANIC:
"[Product name] in glass jar or natural container, warm golden light, 
wooden surface, organic farm aesthetic, Namibian context, ultra realistic"

INPUTS AND SUPPLIES:
"[Product name] in farm setting, practical and clean composition, 
natural light, agricultural context, ultra realistic"

PROCESSED PRODUCTS:
"[Product name], artisan packaging or rustic presentation, warm light, 
wooden or farm surface, Namibian artisan food, ultra realistic"

LANDSHARE AND ENERGY:
"Aerial or wide-angle view of [land type or solar equipment] in 
Namibian landscape, golden light, ultra realistic, documentary style"

Image technical requirements:
- Display size: 400 x 250px per card
- object-fit: cover so it fills the card area cleanly
- Subtle dark gradient overlay on the bottom 30% of every image 
  so badges and text remain readable
- Skeleton loading placeholder shown while each image generates
- Generate all images at build time or on first load only
- Store all generated image URLs in state so images persist 
  through the session and do not regenerate on every page visit
- Each listing gets a unique image — no duplicates across cards
